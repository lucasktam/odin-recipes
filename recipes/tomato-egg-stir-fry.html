Tomato Egg Stir Fry

Back to Main Menu

Description

A classic Cantonese dish I grew up eating. It's a quick weeknight recipe and real flavorful.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut the tomatoes into wedges.
  2. Slice ginger into thin slices, finely mince the garlic.
  3. Beat eggs and set aside.
  4. Heat a pan to high heat. Scramble the eggs in some oil. Set aside once done.
  5. Cook the ginger and garlic in some oil. When cooked, add in the tomatoes and a bit of water.
  6. Season with sugar and some salt and pepper.
