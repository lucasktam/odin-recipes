Sausage Pasta

Back to Main Menu

Description

Another recipe I cooked a lot when I was studying abroad. It's hearty, protein filled, and will warm you up.

Ingredients

  • Pasta (I like Penne)
  • Jarred Red sauce
  • Pork Sausage
  • Bell Pepper
  • Fresh Parsley (optional)
  • Fresh Basil (optional)
  • Onion
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions

  1. Start boiling a pots of water.
  2. Cut up the sausage and then pan fry. When cooked through, set aside.
  3. Start cooking your pasta to al dente.
  4. Dice onion. Start sauteing in some oil. Slice bell pepper into strips and add to pan.
  5. Throw your pasta in there and then throw the sausage in. Put in some jarred red sauce.
  6. Saute for a few more minutes and you're done! Garnish with optional parsley and basil.
